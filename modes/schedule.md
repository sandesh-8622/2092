# Mode: schedule

Manage their schedule in `data/schedule.md`. Two sections: weekly rhythm (recurring patterns) and upcoming (specific dates).

When they mention a class, deadline, exam, event, or recurring commitment, add it. When they ask "what do I have this week" or "when is that thing due," answer from the schedule.

Be proactive with this data. If it's Tuesday night and they have something due Wednesday, mention it during session start. If an exam is 3 days away, bring it up.

Update MEMORY.md if you learn something new about their routine.
